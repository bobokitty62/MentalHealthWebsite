Approximately 1 in 5 adults in the U.S.—43.8 million, or 18.5%—experiences mental illness in a given year.

Depression is the leading cause of disability worldwide, and is a major contributor to the global burden of disease.

90% of those who die by suicide have an underlying mental illness. Suicide is the 10th leading cause of death in the U.S.

Let's talk about mental health.



About

This website is for people to learn about mental health issues.
On this website, you can find educational information to help you understand mental illnesses.
In the "Helping Others" tab you can find ways that you can help your friends and family who are struggling. Lastly, in the "Self-Care" tab you can find ways to care for yourself
