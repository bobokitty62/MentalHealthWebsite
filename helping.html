Helpful Hotlines

  • NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness HelpLine) offers service from Monday to Thursday from 10am - 6pm.
    NAMI offers assistance to people with mental health disorders, their family members, caregivers, and provides info about mental health conditions and refer
    to nearby treatment, support services.
    1-800-950-NAMI (6264)
  • US Department of Veterans Affairs Veterans Crisis Line, 800-273-8255. 24/7 Supports veterans, active service members and loved ones experiencing suicidal thoughts or personal crisis
